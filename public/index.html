how to create a domain on cloudflare
Note: the screenshots were done on 15 March 2026 and go out of date, but the steps should be about the same.

  1. create an account on cloudflare
  2. On the right sidebar click
    Domain registration
    then
    Register domains
  3. search for a new domain name and click the
    Purchase button
  4. if it asks for a plan, I pick the
    Free plan
    at the bottom
  5. fill out the registration information and click the purchase button at the bottom

You have a domain name, congrats!


But you may want to do the following too:
  • I do not click on the
    Prevent bots
    button as I suspect it adds an annoying cloudflare "are you a human" checkbox to your site. Not sure and you might want it.
  • click on the
    configuration tab
  • enable
    DNSSEC
  • on the right sidebar click
    Domains
    , then find your new domain from the list and click the three dot menu and select
    Configure DNS
  • click on the link
    "set up restrictive SPF..."
    to prevent email spoofing
  • on the right sidebar click on
    manage domains
    and
    your new domain
  • click on the
    Submit
    button to add the records
  • on the right, click on the
    Back to Domains
You can view and manage your DNS records by...
  • on the right sidebar click on
    Domains
  • click on
    your new domain

    this overview page has several links to explore
  • to see your DNS recods click on the link that says
    Go to DNS

That should be a good start. I have a page on how to create a website that can use this new domain name.
